About

Why We Chose This Data

This data shows how Ukrainians have been impacted by the Russia-Ukraine war as they have had to flee from their homes. It also shows the lack of resources and access to healthcare they are facing as a result.

Why The Data Is Important

This data is important because gives people a perspective on the wider trends of a war. By showing this data using data visualization, people can easily see the trends occuring and be well-educated about the state of Ukraine so they can help.

Data's Relationship To Worldwide Trends

The entire world is being affected by the Russia-Ukraine war as Ukraines seek refuge in other countries and tensions between countries arise. This data shows how the entire world is being affected through visualizing refugee movement.
